INDOOR AXE THROWING RANGE
SERVING MIAMI & PALMETTO BAY, FL

Welcome to South Florida's first axe throwing range! Axe Habits currently has two convenient locations for you to choose from, one in Downtown Miami and the other in Palmetto Bay.

Each location is unique but both are guaranteed to deliver fantastic service and an unforgettable experience for all participants. Both of our locations have a variety of beer and wine to choose from, as well as complimentary bottled water for when you just need to hydrate.

Whether you are new to the sport of axe throwing or you have tried it before and want to brush up on your skills, we are thrilled to invite you to share a drink and throw some axes with the best!

In addition to our axe throwing lounges, Axe Habits is also available for various events, including corporate events, birthday parties, and team building activities. Looking for the perfect bachelor party idea? Look no further than Axe Habits!

Contact us today to learn more or to book your spot at one of our event venues!
